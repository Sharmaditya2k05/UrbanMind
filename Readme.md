# 🚀 Overview

UrbanMind AI is a research-grade Smart City Digital Twin platform that combines Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Engineering, Simulation, GIS, and Full-Stack Development to create a virtual representation of a modern city.

The platform continuously simulates city operations, analyzes urban data, predicts future trends, and recommends intelligent policies for sustainable city planning.

Unlike traditional dashboards, UrbanMind AI acts as a living digital twin where thousands of interconnected entities evolve over time.
